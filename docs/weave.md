# Soul Weaver

Build a new soul interactively. The form on the left walks through every section the [Authoring Guide](./soul-authoring-guide) and the [10-Axis Radar](./radar-schema) describe; the panel on the right shows your soul's polygon shape and validates as you score.

When all checks pass — required fields filled, [scoring discipline](./soul-authoring-guide#_5-scoring-discipline) honored, three boons + three flaws written — the **Download** button gives you a `<slug>.md` file ready to drop into `docs/souls/`. **Copy markdown** writes the same content to your clipboard. Drafts auto-save to your browser's localStorage; refresh or come back tomorrow without losing work.

Want a head start? Use the **Preset radar from existing soul** dropdown in the Radar Scores section to copy any of the 9 starter souls' scores into the form, then tune from there.
